Exploring Gross Domestic Product and Spending:
Military, Healthcare and Education

The purpose of this project is to analyze the military, education and healthcare expenditure of United States, United Kingdom, Brazil, Russia, Japan, Canada, China, France, Germany and Australia, for the time period, 2014-2018 through the following -

  •  Comparison of a country's spending to its' GDP
  •  Comparison of a country's education and health expenditure to its' military expenditure
  •  Comparison of a country's per capita educational expenditure to its' per capita GDP
  •  Comparison of a country's per capita healthcare spending to its' per capita GDP
  •  Comparison of a country's per capita military spending to its' per capita GDP
  •  Fastest growing countries in healthcare and educational spending in absolute and percentage values
